Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SmartSelective

When will I be charged?

Your credit card will be charged $99 immediately when you sign up. After that, you'll be charged $99 on the same date each month. Cancel anytime, no fees.

Which schools is this designed for?

SmartSelective VIC Edition is specifically designed for students preparing for Melbourne High School, Mac.Robertson Girls' High School, Nossal High School, and Suzanne Cory High School entrance exams.

What subjects are covered?

Every weekly test includes all 4 subjects tested in the VIC Selective Schools exam: Numerical Reasoning, Reading Comprehension, Mathematics, and Verbal Reasoning.

Are the questions repeated each week?

No! Every test features completely new questions. We generate fresh content every week, so your child never sees the same question twice.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click - no cancellation fees, no questions asked.

How are the tests delivered?

Tests are delivered via email every Monday at 4am. You'll receive a PDF file that you can print or complete digitally.

Do you provide answer explanations?

Yes! Every test includes complete answer keys with detailed explanations to help your child understand the concepts and learn from mistakes.

What if I sign up mid-month?

You'll get immediate access to your welcome bonus test, plus your first Monday delivery. Your billing cycle starts from your signup date.

Is there a family discount?

Not at this time, but one subscription covers unlimited practice for your household. Multiple children can use the same weekly tests.
