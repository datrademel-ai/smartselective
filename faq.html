Preparation Tips

Ideally, start 16-20 weeks before the exam (late February or early March for a June exam). This gives time for baseline assessment, skill building, consistent improvement, and peak confidence by exam day. Starting later is still beneficial - even 8-10 weeks of practice makes a significant difference.

We recommend:
• Complete in one 2-hour sitting when possible (builds stamina)
• Use proper timing (30 min per subject)
• Simulate exam conditions (quiet space, no distractions)
• Mark immediately after completing
• Review all explanations thoroughly
• Identify weak areas and practice those during the week
