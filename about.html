Our Story

SmartSelective was born from a simple observation: families were spending $3,000-5,000 on selective school test preparation, yet often still felt underprepared. The problem wasn't the dedication of students or parents - it was the cost and repetitive nature of traditional tutoring.

As parents ourselves, we saw firsthand how expensive tutoring became prohibitive for many families, while those who could afford it often found their children memorizing answers from repeated practice books rather than truly learning.

We asked ourselves: What if there was a better way? What if we could provide fresh, high-quality practice every week at a fraction of traditional costs? What if technology could make excellent preparation materials accessible to everyone?

That question led to SmartSelective. By combining advanced AI with educational expertise, we create brand-new practice tests weekly - no repetition, no memorization, just genuine skill-building. And by delivering digitally, we eliminate the overhead costs of physical locations and in-person classes.

Today, SmartSelective helps over 120 families prepare for VIC Selective Schools at $99/month - less than the cost of a single week of tutoring.
