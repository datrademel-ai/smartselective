About SmartSelective

Making quality selective schools test preparation accessible to all families

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves access to high-quality test preparation, regardless of their family's budget. SmartSelective was created to make selective schools exam preparation affordable, convenient, and effective.

Why We Started SmartSelective

As parents and educators, we saw firsthand how expensive and time-consuming traditional tutoring could be. Many families were spending $400-600 per month on group classes, with limited flexibility and often repetitive materials.

We knew there had to be a better way. That's why we created SmartSelective - to provide fresh, high-quality practice tests every week at a fraction of the cost of traditional tutoring.

Our Commitment

Every test we create is designed by experienced educators who understand the VIC Selective Schools exam format. We ensure that:

  • Questions are fresh and unique every week
  • Content aligns with the actual exam format
  • Difficulty levels are appropriate and progressive
  • Explanations are clear and educational
