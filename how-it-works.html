The Detailed Version

Everything you need to know about using SmartSelective

1

Sign Up & Get Started

Visit our signup page and create your account in under 60 seconds. You'll need:

  • Email address
  • Password
  • Credit card for payment

Billing starts immediately at $99/month. Your card will be charged right away, then on the same date each month thereafter.

Welcome Bonus: Immediately after payment, you'll receive an email with your first complete practice test. No waiting - start practicing right away!

üí° Tip: Download and save your welcome test immediately. Print it or keep it digital - whatever works best for your family.

2

Receive Weekly Tests

Every Monday at 4:00 AM AEST, a new practice test arrives in your inbox. Each email contains:

  • Direct download link to the week's test (PDF format)
  • Reminder of your progress so far
  • Tips for effective practice

Each test includes:

  • Questions booklet (all 4 subjects, 85-90 questions total)
  • Answer key with correct answers
  • Detailed explanations document

File size: Typically 1-2MB total - quick to download even on mobile data.

üí° Tip: Add hello@smartselective.com.au to your contacts to ensure emails don't go to spam.

3

Complete the Test

Recommended approach:

  • Set aside 2 hours in one sitting (mimics real exam conditions)
  • Use a quiet space with minimal distractions
  • Use a timer - 30 minutes per subject
  • Complete under exam conditions - no help, no breaks mid-section

Flexible options: If 2 hours is too much at once, you can split by subject. Just maintain the 30-minute time limit for each section.

Print or digital? Either works! Many families print for authenticity, others prefer completing digitally. Whatever helps your child focus best.

üí° Tip: Schedule your weekly test time in advance - Saturdays 10am works well for many families. Consistency helps build routine.

4

Mark & Review

Immediately after completing the test:

  • Use the answer key to mark each question
  • Calculate your score for each subject
  • Identify which questions were incorrect

Review the explanations:

  • Read explanations for ALL questions - even ones you got right (confirms understanding)
  • Pay special attention to incorrect answers - understand WHY you got them wrong
  • Note patterns - are errors in a specific subject or question type?

Track your progress: Keep a simple spreadsheet or notebook with weekly scores. Seeing improvement is motivating!

üí° Tip: Review should happen the same day or next day while questions are fresh in memory. Don't wait a week!

5

Practice Weak Areas

Between weekly tests:

  • If Numerical Reasoning was weak ‚Üí Practice pattern recognition online or in workbooks
  • If Reading was weak ‚Üí Read more challenging texts (newspapers, novels)
  • If Math was weak ‚Üí Review school textbooks, practice mental arithmetic
  • If Verbal Reasoning was weak ‚Üí Play word games, expand vocabulary

You have unlimited access to all past tests - go back and retry questions from previous weeks if needed.

üí° Tip: SmartSelective provides the testing framework. Combine with school homework, reading, and targeted practice for comprehensive preparation.

Typical Preparation Timeline

March to June: Your 16-week journey

Week 1-2

Getting Started

Baseline assessment. Don't worry about scores - these first tests establish where you're starting from. Focus on getting comfortable with format and timing.

Week 3-6

Building Skills

Steady improvement should begin. Identify consistent weak areas and focus extra practice there. Develop exam strategies and time management.

Week 7-12

Peak Performance

Scores should be significantly higher than week 1. Confidence building. Fine-tuning weak areas. Maintaining consistent practice routine.

Week 13-16

Final Preparation

Maintain practice but reduce stress. Review challenging question types. Focus on confidence and mental readiness. Taper intensity in final week.
